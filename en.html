ОО «ECO LIFE REVIVAL GROUP» IS A KAZAKHSTANI ENVIRONMENTAL COMPANY PROVIDING A FULL COMPLEX OF WORKS IN THE FIELD OF ECOLOGY, GEOLOGY, SANITARY, ANTI-EPIDEMIC ACTIVITIES, RADIATION, INDUSTRIAL SAFETY AND THE MINING, CHEMICAL AND OIL AND GAS INDUSTRY.

Our service

ENVIRONMENTAL DESIGN AND REGULATION Environmental design – development of all types of planning and project documentation, other materials and documents or their individual sections and parts relating to environmental protection and environmental management, subject to mandatory state environmental expertise, in accordance with the Environmental code of The Republic of Kazakhstan
ECOLOGICAL EXPERTISE Ecological expertise – this is the establishment of compliance of the planned economic activity with other environmental quality standards and environmental requirements, as well as the determination of the admissibility of the object of environmental expertise in order to prevent possible adverse effects of this activity on the environment and related social consequences.On the basis of the license No. 00975R, for performance of works and rendering services in issued by the Ministry of environmental protection of RK of 20.06.2007, the ОО «ECO LIFE REVIVAL GROUP» company provides services in carrying out ecological examination.
ENVIRONMENTAL AUDIT Environmental audit independent audit of economic and other activities of the audited entities aimed at identifying and assessing environmental risks and developing recommendations to improve the environmental safety of their activities. Environmental company "ОО «ECO LIFE REVIVAL GROUP» & Co" LLP carries out an initiative environmental audit of enterprises and organizations in the certification of environmental requirements, development and implementation of investment projects, licensing of subsoil use or hazardous waste management, as well as for the development or revision of environmental payments of the enterprise.
INDUSTRIAL SAFETY Our company will be glad to provide you with assistance and cooperation in the field of industrial safety. In our time, industrial safety is becoming increasingly important in the light of the increasing number of environmental and man-made disasters. In this regard, industrial safety is a top priority for any owner. Industrial safety – an area where there are clearly established rules and regulations, compliance with the characteristics of the object which confirms the examination of industrial safety. Industrial safety imposes certain requirements to the object of expertise.
CERTIFICATION OF WORKPLACES ОО «ECO LIFE REVIVAL GROUP» & co LLP carries out activities on certification of production facilities in accordance with the rules of certification of production facilities under working conditions, approved by the government of the Republic of Kazakhstan dated March 31, 2015. No. 185 , and also carries out production control of harmful factors of the working environment, the State license No. 0145754 of 14.06.2010 of ОО «ECO LIFE REVIVAL GROUP» LLP is brought in the List of the Specialized organizations and it is placed on the web-site www.enbek.gov.kz
Conducting environmental actions and events ECO LIFE REVIVAL GROUP NGO The organization regularly conducts educational programs and trainings for various target audiences, including schoolchildren, students, company employees and the public, the organization organizes various environmental actions and events such as cleaning territories, planting trees and environmental festivals.
